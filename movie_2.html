The Hunt for the Best South American Cuisine in the World

Are you ready to experience the most amazing foods in the world for the second time?

Well, we are here in South America to give you that experience!

We go from place to place to try out some of the best foods and are astonished by everything we see !

Directed by Paola Chamorro, with Vicky Huang as screenplay writer, The Hunt for the Best South American Cuisine in the world seeks to answer the age long answer of which South American country in the large continent holds the best food pallet and dives deep for exquisit never before seen dishes which will enchant American viewers

Times calls the film an "magnificient masterpiece, exploring all the new rage in South American Cuisine and opening up the world to the South American culture through the use of food in this second spectacular film"
